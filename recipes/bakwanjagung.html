Bakwan Jagung Recipe

Bakwan Jagung is an Indonesian snack. It is made of flour dough mixed with corn kernels, it will be fried in spherical shapes

Ingredients

  • 3 sweet corns
  • 350 gr flour
  • 125 gr rice flour
  • 4 garlics, blend until smooth
  • 8 red onion, chopped smoothly
  • 3 chopped chives
  • 1 chopped celery
  • 2 chilies
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • salt and pepper
  • water

Steps

  1. Comb sweet corn using a knife. Use corn kernels only
  2. Slice spring onions, celery, chilies and shallots. Put it in the corn that has been combed earlier in 1 container
  3. Grind the garlic, put it in the corn container
  4. Put both types of flour in 1 container, also add garlic powder, onion powder, baking powder, add enough water. combine with corn mixture. stir well, add salt and pepper
  5. Heat cooking oil, fry until golden brown, remove
  6. Finished!
